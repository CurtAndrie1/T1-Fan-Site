Lee Sang-hyeok:

FAKER close

Lee "Faker" Sang-hyeok is the most decorated League of Legends player. He is a 28 year old Korean, playing for T1 as a Midlaner.

Faker has won five World Championships (2013, 2015, 2016, 2023, 2024 the most of any player) and two Mid-Season Invitationals (2016, 2017), and has 10 combined LCK and Champions Korea titles. He is sometimes referred to as "The Unkillable Demon King" by Korean fans.

On May 22nd, 2024, Faker was announced as the inaugural inductee into the Hall of Legends by LoL Esports. He was officially inducted at The Shilla Seoul in South Korea on June 6th, 2024.
